📥 INWARD (Stock In - Auto Stock Calc)

📊 Auto Calculations:
• Net Stock = Challan Qty - Shortage - Damage + Excess
• Stock Status = Good Stock / Expiry Soon / Stock Expiry (based on Expiry Date)
• No of Days to Expire = Auto calculated from Expiry Date
• Remarks = Good stock / Shortage / Damage / Excess (based on quantities)

📤 OUTWARD (Dispatch - Auto TAT)

📊 Auto TAT Formulas:
• Dispatches TAT = Shiprocket date - Order Picked up Date
• Delivered TAT = Actual Order Delivered Date - Shiprocket date
• Blank values allowed - will be saved as blank
